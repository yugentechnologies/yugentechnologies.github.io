ABOUT US

WHAT WE DO

We are a technology start-up based in Madhya Pradesh, founded by Jay Kumar and Manu Bhadoria.

Our experts have over 2 decades of experience in various technological fields with successful projects in multiple government departments.

We serve a variety of industries including but not limited to agriculture, forestry, water resources, railways, land administration, government, engineering and survey. Through sustainable research and innovation, we aspire to be one of the top global brands in technological development.

GEOGRAPHICAL INFORMATION SYSTEMS

We have deep knowledge of the geospatial domain. We offer consumers a wide range of products and services from Mobile GIS , Desktop GIS to Server GIS solutions. In addition to being a professional GIS vendor, we also provide a wide variety of services, including: GIS project services, Professional GIS education training, Consultancy services, Cloud GIS projects and Mobile applications for iOS/Android phones.
